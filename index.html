Why choose my portraits

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book and what does it cost?

Prices are by size, with custom sizes available. To save your spot, make a 2000 SEK deposit (this goes toward your total). See current sizes and rates on Prices & How It Works.

What styles do you offer?

I paint detailed oil portraits and a new painterly wet-on-wet style (expressive brushwork, limited launch pricing). Note: detailed oil pricing includes free shipping; the launch offer does not include shipping.

What payment schedule do you use?

Deposit: 2000 SEK to join the queue. Before I start: up to 50% (includes your deposit). Final balance: after you approve the finished portrait.

How long does it take and can you meet a deadline?

Commissions run on a first-come, first-served schedule. Shipping is door-to-door—typically a few days within Europe and about 3-7 days worldwide (can be longer around Christmas). If you have a date in mind, contact me for current availability.

What photos do you need?

Your photos are pivotal, please send large, sharp, well-lit images. I'm happy to help you choose. See Photo Tips or ask me for a quick consultation.

Will I see progress and can I request tweaks?

Yes. I share work-in-progress updates so you can give feedback as the portrait develops. Minor finishing touches can be requested at the end.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancel within 7 days of ordering for a full refund. After 7 days or once work starts, a 20-50% fee applies to cover time and materials.

Do you accept returns or offer refunds?

Custom art is non-returnable and non-refundable. If your portrait arrives damaged or incorrect, I will remake it free of charge - just report it within 7 days with photos of the packaging and artwork. See the full Return & Refund Policy.

Do you ship worldwide? What about costs and currency?

Yes, worldwide shipping via trusted carriers (e.g., FedEx Express). Detailed oil pricing includes free shipping; the painterly wet-on-wet launch pricing excludes shipping. Prices are listed in SEK - use the currency converter on the Prices page for an estimate.

Is this a good gift if I don't have photos yet?

Yes, choose a Gift Voucher so the recipient can provide photos later. Packaging and shipping for the voucher are free. Contact me to arrange.

Who owns the copyright? Can I share my portrait online?

Copyright remains with the artist. You're welcome to display your portrait at home and share photos online for personal use; commercial reproduction requires written permission. See Terms & Privacy.

How do I start?

Message me with your favorite photos and size ideas, or just your budget. I'll suggest the best option and reserve your spot. Join My Client List

About the artist

Meet Katja - Pet Portrait Artist with Heart

I'm a fine artist based near Gothenburg, Sweden. For over 25 years, I've helped families memorialize their closest companions in oil.

My process is personal, collaborative, and quietly obsessive about the small things like whiskers that catch the light, a particular tilt of the head, the glint of recognition in the eye.
