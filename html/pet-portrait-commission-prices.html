Customized Oil Portraits to Fit Your Vision

Every portrait is painted by hand in oil, from your photos, with a focus on character and emotion. You can choose between two styles:

  • Painterly Oils - expressive, wet-on-wet brushwork with visible strokes and a softer, more atmospheric look.
  • Detailed Signature Oils - highly refined realism with fine layering and close attention to every tiny feature.

Pricing is based mainly on canvas size and style. If you have specific dimensions or a special idea in mind, I can adapt the canvas within practical limits. I'll guide you personally from the first email to the final completed oil painting - helping you choose the right style, size, and composition before any paint touches the canvas.

Choosing Your Style: Painterly or Detailed?

Not sure which style is right for you? Here's a clear side-by-side comparison to make the choice easier.

Painterly Oils - expressive & more accessible

  • Visible brushstrokes, movement and atmosphere
  • A high-end original at a more accessible price
  • Artistic, slightly looser interpretation with strong character
  • Includes free worldwide shipping

Detailed Signature Oils - premium & limited

  • Built up in multiple layers for maximum realism
  • Every whisker, highlight and texture rendered with care
  • Ideal for memorial portraits and “forever pieces”
  • Includes free worldwide shipping and very limited yearly slots

If you're unsure, simply send me your favourite photos and tell me a bit about your pet and your budget. I'll suggest a style and size that make sense for you and where the painting will hang.

How the Commission Process Works

The “best” size depends on a few factors:

  • How many pets you'd like to include
  • Whether you prefer a head-and-shoulders portrait or full body
  • Where the painting will hang and what feels comfortable budget-wise

Most clients start by choosing a style (Painterly or Detailed) and then selecting one of the sizes above as a starting point. From there, we refine the composition together.

Step 1 - Join the client list

To reserve a place in my schedule, you join my client list with a 2 000 SEK deposit.

Step 2 - Photos & planning

You send your favourite reference photos (or I help you choose). We discuss your ideas, preferred style, size and any special details you'd like included. I then create a digital mock-up so you can see the overall composition before I begin painting.

Step 3 - Painting & updates

Once everything is confirmed, I begin painting your portrait. Detailed commissions are built up in multiple layers, while painterly pieces are created more directly, wet-on-wet. You'll receive regular email updates with photos so you can follow your portrait's progress.

Step 4 - Approval & delivery

When the portrait is finished, you receive photos to approve. Minor adjustments can be made at this stage if needed. Once you're happy, the final balance is paid and your painting is prepared for shipping.

Partial Payments

  • 2 000 SEK client list deposit
  • Up to 50% before painting starts (deposit included in this)
  • Remaining balance after you approve the finished portrait

You don't need to decide every detail on day one - we'll fine-tune the size and composition together after I've seen your photos.

Your Photos Matter

Enhancing Your Pet's Essence with High-Quality Photos

Your photos are crucial to the outcome.

Because I usually can't meet your pet in person, I rely entirely on your references '' large, sharp, well-lit images produce the best results.

  • Choose photos where your pet's eyes are clear and sharp
  • Avoid heavy filters or very dark/overexposed images
  • If possible, send several angles so I can get to know their personality

You can read more in my Photo Tips section, or contact me for a short photo consultation if you're not sure which images to use.

Are You Buying a Portrait as a Gift?

Yes - you can absolutely order a portrait as a gift, even if you don't have photos yet or are short on time for a special occasion.

Shipping & Availability

If you have a specific deadline (birthday, anniversary, Christmas), please contact me as early as possible for current delivery dates.

Portraits are carefully packed and shipped with door-to-door delivery with FedEx. Once shipped, delivery is typically:

  • A few days within Europe
  • Around 3-7 days worldwide

Please note that shipping can take longer around Christmas and other busy seasons. For Christmas portraits or time-sensitive gifts, it's always best to book early - especially for detailed signature oils.
