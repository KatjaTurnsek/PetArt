Pet Portrait Commission Pricing and How It Works

Customized Oil Portrait Commissions to Fit Your Vision

When it comes to pet portrait commissions, the cost is determined by the size of your choice. Whether you have specific dimensions in mind or unique requests, I can customize the canvas size to perfectly suit your needs. However, certain limitations may apply depending on your preferences.

Rest assured that I prioritize meeting all my clients' requirements and wishes on a personal level. In fact, I go above and beyond to help you envision your final oil painting even before I begin applying the first stroke onto the blank canvas. Understanding How Pet Portrait Commissions Work

Are you wondering which size would be the best fit for your portrait? Well, the answer depends on a few factors.

One consideration could be the number of pets you want to include in your painting. Another factor could be something as simple as your budget.

To determine the appropriate size for your portrait, begin by selecting your preferred price range. To get started, just join my client list and make a deposit of 2000 SEK.

Once I have completed your mock-up, we can discuss the right size and dimensions that suit your needs.

Partial Payments

  • Client list deposit
  • Up to 50% before I start (including client list deposit)
  • Balance payment once the portrait is approved by you

Now, isn't that great!? No need to stress about sizes right now.

Enhancing Your Pet's Essence with High-Quality Photos

When it comes to commissioning a pet portrait, your photo plays a pivotal role in ensuring the quality of my work.

Since I usually lack the opportunity to meet and personally photograph the pets I paint, I heavily rely on the photos you provide.

The significance of your photo cannot be overstated; the higher the quality of the photos I receive, the better your portrait will turn out.

It is crucial that these photos are large, sharp, and clear. Consider selecting some of the finest shots of your beloved pet or even capturing new ones if feasible.

As we discuss your pet portrait commission, remember that investing in high-quality images will greatly contribute to an exceptional final result.

For more information about photos, view my Photo Tips. Unsure about which photo to choose? Contact me for a consultation.
