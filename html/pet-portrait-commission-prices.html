Understanding How Pet Portrait Commissions Work

The “best” size depends on a few factors:

How many pets you want to include, the composition you prefer, and your budget.

Start by picking a preferred price range. To join my queue, simply join my client list by making a 2000 SEK deposit.

After I prepare a mock-up, we'll confirm the ideal size and proportions for your piece.

Partial Payments

  • Client list deposit
  • Up to 50% before I start (includes the deposit)
  • Balance after you approve the finished portrait

No stress about exact sizes up front—we'll dial it in together.

Enhancing Your Pet's Essence with High-Quality Photos

Your photos are pivotal to the outcome.

Because I usually can't meet your pet in person, I rely entirely on your references—large, sharp, and well-lit images produce the best results.

Select the finest shots of your pet (or take fresh ones, if possible). Investing in quality references directly improves the final painting.

See my Photo Tips. Not sure which photo to use? Contact me for a consultation.
