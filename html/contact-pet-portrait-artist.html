How to Book

  1. Email or use the form with a few photos (links are fine).
  2. We confirm the concept (size, style: classic or painterly, background).
  3. Reserve your date with a 2,000 SEK deposit (deducted from the total).
  4. Paint & updates - I'll share work-in-progress for your feedback.
  5. Approve & ship - varnished, securely packed, shipped worldwide.

What to Include

  • Best photos (or links) - eyes sharp, natural light if possible
  • Preferred size (or a budget range)
  • Style: classic realism or painterly
  • Any deadline or gift date

Good to Know

  • Deposit: 2,000 SEK to reserve (PayPal/cards accepted)
  • Timing: first-come-first-served; gifts welcome - ask for availability
  • Shipping: worldwide, tracked; careful archival packaging
  • Materials: museum-grade oils on fine linen canvas
