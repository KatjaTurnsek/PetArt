Commissioned Pet Portraits in Oils: A Gallery of Exquisite Paintings

Custom-Painted from Your Photos

I specialize in creating exquisite commissioned pet portraits, meticulously hand-painted using oils on professionally stretched gallery-wrapped linen canvas. Whether you're looking for dog portraits, cat portraits, or horse portraits, I can bring your beloved pets to life through my art.

Your commissioned hand painted oil painting will be a one-of-a-kind masterpiece that is completely custom-made. I will work closely with you to bring your vision to reality, using your favorite photo as a reference and incorporating any ideas or special requests you may have. From the orientation of the portrait to the suitable backgrounds, colors, and painting size, everything will be tailored to your preferences.

Painted Year 2020-2024

A Work of Art: Not Just A Copy of Your Photo

My pet portraits are meticulously painted with dynamic movement and an abundance of expression. From broad, visible brushstrokes to intricate details, each painting is a true work of art that goes beyond simply reproducing your photograph.

Using oils as my medium, I strive to create expressive and lifelike oil portraits that showcase both the strength of brushwork and the importance of capturing every detail where necessary. Rest assured; you will not receive a mare copy of your photo but a unique piece that embodies the spirit of your beloved pet.

My ultimate goal I to not only capture your furry friend's distinct characteristics and likeness but also infuse the painting with vibrancy, vitality and luminosity. With my hand-painted approach, you can expect nothing less than a stunning pet portrait created in my signature style.

Painted Year 2017-2019

Pet Portraits in Oils: Painting Techniques

To create stunning pet portraits in oils, I begin by using acrylics and pre-mixing the oil paints. This allows me to achieve a limited palette that enhances color harmony, depth, and light in my oil painting pet portraits.

For the highest quality results, I exclusively work with hand-painted techniques on premium linen canvases. These canvases are gallery-wrapped and stretched to wooden stretcher bars for a truly professional finish.
